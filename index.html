About

When data expertise and cow sense come in the same package.

Dr. V is a veterinarian specialized in epidemiology, and a board-certified preventive medicine specialist — a resource dairy farms call when the data flags a problem and the cause isn’t obvious.

Most dairies have data. Few have answers. That gap is where the work happens. Whether it is reproductive efficiency, involuntary culling, or herd health events that defy explanation — the data gets followed until it points to exactly where to look first.

Founded in 2008. Trained at UC Davis and Colorado State University. Experience spanning small dairies to operations of 10,000+ cows across three continents. Now working for yours.

DVM (1996) · MPVM (2002) · PhD in Epidemiology (2007) DACVPM — Diplomate, American College of Veterinary Preventive Medicine FADD — Foreign Animal Disease Diagnostician, USDA (2024) Author — Practical Clinical Epidemiology for the Veterinarian (Wiley, 2015) 250+ conference presentations and publications worldwide
